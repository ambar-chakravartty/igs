The Cybernetic Sprint

TYPE: Team (One Team per School) NO. OF PARTICIPANTS: 3 per Team MODE: Offline GRADE: 9-12 DATE: 28 July, 2022 DURATION: 3 Hours (10am - 1pm) Description: Participants will be given the task of making a Robot (ground based, no aerial aspect allowed). The Robot will have to navigate a track. The objective is for the Robot to complete the track in the least amount of time with the least number of mistakes The Robot would not be controlled by the participants while on track. It must have capabilities of an autonomous control to navigate the track without continuous human inputs The Robot has to be made by the participants of the team themselves before arriving for the Competition Use of any kind of robotic kits or ready-to-make sets is prohibited and will lead to disqualification of the Team The use of Microcontrollers (for example Arduino) is allowed for autonomous control of the Robot. But other High End Boards (like RaspberryPi) and IOT based Circuit Boards (like NodeMCU) are not allowed A DC power supply needs to be used for powering the robot and it should be an integral part of the robot. The chassis of the Robot can be 3D printed, made out of wood, or made out of metal but direct use of a fully developed chassis with motors and circuitry for making the Robot is prohibited. The basic circuitry of the Robot including breadboard/ PCB, motors, sensors, wheel are permitted The Participants have to get their own laptop and hardware for making any changes to the program and fine tuning the robot at the venue before the competition begins
